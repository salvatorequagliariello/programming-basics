(inner) create_spend_chart(…categories) → {string}

Creates a graphic chart containing a visual representation of the of the percentage spent in every Category object passed into the function. The percentage spent is calculated only with withdrawals, from 0 to 100. The bars in the chart are made out of the o character. Uses loops to iterate over objects properties. The string it returns is logically divided between two section, the upper part and the lower part.
